Good Friend And Good Food

Eat with me is an application that links you up with people share the same interest and the same schedule and free time as yours.

It will allow you to go out and eat together with people other than eating alone. You can pair with a random person or invite people that you would like to eat out with them. The website will contact the restaurant and make a reservation for you once the group conforms their availability.
